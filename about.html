Our Story

Founded in 2023 by Jahid Ansari, NexGenAiTech emerged from a vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge artificial intelligence and robust full-stack development. With 2+ years of hands-on experience in the tech industry, Jahid identified a critical need for integrated solutions that combine AI's transformative power with scalable software architecture.

What started as a solo venture has rapidly evolved into a dedicated team of passionate developers, AI specialists, and UX designers, all united by a common goal: to deliver exceptional digital solutions that drive real business impact.

Today, NexGenAiTech serves clients across various industries, from ambitious startups to established enterprises, helping them navigate the digital transformation journey with confidence and clarity.
